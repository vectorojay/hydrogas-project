About Us

Welcome to Hydropet Oil Services Limited, a wholly indigenous company founded in 2011 and one of the leading names in the oil and gas industry and marketers of petroleum product such as petrol (PMS), diesel (AGO), kerosene (DKP) and cooking gas (LPG) to both retail and bulk users.

Over the years, we have constantly evolved by implementing new ideas and set high deals. And in the process, we have contributed towards a modern, stylish and technologically advanced Retail Outlets in Port Harcourt Nigeria.

Our Vision

To be the foremost retail brand in the oil and gas business. delivering quick and excellent petroleum product by increasing market share index through the use of state-of-the art facility with reputable sales network.

Mission Statement

  • To bring petroleum products to the final customer.
  • To maintain a reputable standard in the oil and gas sector.
  • To ease the movement of petroleum products from refineries.
