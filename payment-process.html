Enjoy Seamless Payment Processes

Guide to help you navigate through our online payment processes. Follow these
steps to make your purchases securely and efficiently.

  • 1. Add items to your cart:

    Browse through our selection of gas products and add the items you wish to purchase to your shopping cart by clicking the "Add to Cart" button.

  • 2. Review Your Cart:

    Once you have added all the desired items, click on the shopping cart icon located in the upper right-hand corner of the website to review your order.

  • 3. Enter Your Information:

    Fill in your billing and shipping information accurately. Make sure to provide the correct details to ensure a smooth delivery process. Choose your preferred shipping method and review the shipping costs.

  • 4. Select Payment Method:

    Choose your preferred payment method from the list of available options. We currently only accept the following payment methods: Credit Card payment method & Transfer payment method.

  • 5. Enter Payment Information:

    If you choose to pay by credit/debit card, enter your card number, expiration date, CVV, and billing address. Click "Continue" to proceed. This option is unavailable for now.

  • 6. Proceed to Checkout:

    Click the "Proceed to Checkout" button to begin the checkout process.

OR

You can also click on the chatbot at the right bottom corner of the page . A form will pop up. Fill in your full name, email address and details of your order, specifying the products you want and their description.

Click on the submit button when done.

A Representative would be in touch with you shortly.

OR

You can walk into any of our branches nationwide, pick up your order and pay using Cash Payments, POS, or Bank Transfer options.
